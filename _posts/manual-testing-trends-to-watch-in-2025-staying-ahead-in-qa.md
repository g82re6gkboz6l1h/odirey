## Why Manual Testing Matters in 2025

Unlike automated testing, manual testing leverages human capacity to assess software functionality, usability, and user experience. It retains its importance in:

- **Exploratory Testing**: Automated systems cannot replicate the creative problem-solving of human testers.
- **User-Centric Validation**: Identifying usability and accessibility nuances often requires manual intervention.
- **New Technology Testing**: As innovations like generative AI emerge, manual testers ensure outcomes are ethical and user-friendly.

A balanced approach combining manual and automated testing will define QA strategies in 2025.
