## Introduction to Manual Testing in 2025

Manual testing involves executing test cases without automation, focusing on areas like usability, exploratory testing, and understanding user behaviors. While automation has accelerated testing cycles, 2025 showcases how manual testing complements automation, especially in workflows requiring human intuition and creativity.

Key trends driving manual testing include:
- Complex user interfaces demanding nuanced analysis.
- Rapid iteration in SaaS products.
- Hyper-personalized customer experiences that demand manual evaluation.
