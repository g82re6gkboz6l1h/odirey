## Writing Impactful Test Cases: 6 Tips

1. **Understand Requirements** thoroughly before drafting.
2. **Use Simplicity** to ensure clarity for all stakeholders.
3. **Account for Diverse Scenarios**—positive, negative, and edge cases.
4. **Prioritize Reusability** to streamline future projects.
5. **Implement Traceability** to measure efficiency.
6. **Optimize Regularly** to meet evolving requirements.

Well-crafted test cases increase collaboration and boost QA efficiency.
