## Key Strategies for Proficient Manual Testing

Help your team stay ahead of testing challenges by adopting these groundbreaking strategies:

### 1. Context-Driven Testing
Every app or system is unique. With **context-driven testing**, QA teams personalize strategies based on specific software functions, user expectations, and potential project risks.

### 2. Risk-Based Testing
Focus on high-priority areas by directing efforts toward components with the most significant potential risks. This ensures essential features are highly dependable while reducing wasted resources.

### 3. Enhanced Exploratory Testing
Encourage testers to adopt advanced exploratory testing techniques, such as **session-based testing**, blending creativity with structured efforts. These methods excel in identifying unpredictable issues in complex systems.

### 4. Collaboration & Pair Testing
Engage developers and product owners by integrating manual testers into collaborative pair testing sessions. Early-stage insights improve functionality and focus the test strategy on critical features.

### 5. AI-Driven Assistance
