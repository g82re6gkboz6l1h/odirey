## Why Manual Testing Still Matters

Despite AI, here’s why manual testing remains essential:
1. **Exploratory Testing**: Humans detect subtle, contextual bugs AI might miss.
2. **User Experience Assessment**: Only humans truly grasp intuitive designs.
3. **Adaptability**: Manual testers adjust to unforeseen developments better.
4. **Critical Thinking & Ethics**: Humans evaluate edge cases and moral impacts.

Manual testing complements AI-driven automation to ensure well-rounded software quality.
