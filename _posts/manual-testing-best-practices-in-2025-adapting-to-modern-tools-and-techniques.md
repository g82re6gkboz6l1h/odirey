## Challenges Faced by Manual Testers in Modern Times

Manual testers now operate in increasingly complex environments. Here are key challenges to address:

1. **Handling Complex Software**: Applications powered by AI, machine learning (ML), and cloud computing demand advanced domain expertise during testing.
2. **Shortened Release Cycles**: Agile and DevOps workflows push QA teams to deliver quality within tight deadlines.
3. **Diverse User Scenarios**: Testing across multiple devices, operating systems, and environments introduces edge cases automation may miss.
4. **AI-Powered Systems**: Thorough manual testing for AI-driven applications requires understanding unpredictable, dynamic behaviors.

By adopting refined strategies and integrating advanced tools, manual testers can overcome obstacles and excel in delivering optimal results.
