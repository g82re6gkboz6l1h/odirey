## Emerging Technologies Driving the Future of Manual Testing

The advent of next-gen technology has reshaped how manual testing is conducted:
- **Cloud Systems**: Focuses on multi-cloud performance validation.
- **IoT Devices**: Integrates testing across device connectivity challenges.
- **AR/VR Use Cases**: Manual insights excel in testing immersive experiences.
- **Blockchain Standards**: Ensures flawless integrity and safe asset verification.
- **Microservices Architecture**: Works on validation layers critical for API workflows.
AI is also extending opportunities for a smarter workflow.
