## Why Manual Testing Matters in AI-transformed QA

AI elevates QA workflows by detecting anomalies, running endless test cases, and providing faster scalability. Yet, when it comes to evaluating intuitive experiences like usability, emotional design, and accessibility, manual testing holds an irreplaceable position. Human insight is critical for:

- Spotting inconsistencies in the user interface (UI).
- Addressing ethical and emotional responses.
- Testing edge cases or non-standard user behaviors.

While AI tools excel at processing large data sets, manual testing bridges the gap between cold automation and human user experiences. In safety-driven industries, like autonomous vehicles or healthcare, manual QA ensures user trust by handling unique, nuanced situations more effectively.
