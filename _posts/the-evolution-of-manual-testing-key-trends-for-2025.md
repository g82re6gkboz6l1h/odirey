## **Introduction**

Manual testing prioritizes the human touch, focusing on user empathy, exploratory analysis, and qualitative nuances that automation tools often overlook. This unique angle is irreplaceable for assessing user experience (UX), accessibility, and app functionality. As we look ahead, manual testers will adapt to multimodal interfaces, advanced AI tools, and complex environments like AR apps and IoT devices.
