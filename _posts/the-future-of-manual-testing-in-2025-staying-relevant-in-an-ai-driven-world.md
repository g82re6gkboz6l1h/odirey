The software testing industry is rapidly evolving as artificial intelligence (AI) and automation gain prominence. Despite advancements in technology, manual testing remains essential to ensure user-centric software quality and identify unique issues automation cannot. This blog explores the future of manual testing in 2025, emphasizing how testers can stay relevant by leveraging AI-driven tools like Zof AI, developing soft skills, focusing on exploratory testing, and building expertise in niche domains. Learn how manual testing complements automation and how tools such as Zof AI empower testers to add value in the tech-driven landscape of the future.
