## Key Technical Skills for 2025

### 1. **Automation Framework Basics**
Understanding frameworks like Selenium and Appium enhances collaboration with automation engineers. Familiarity with coding aspects like Cypress or JavaScript will further boost your expertise.

### 2. **API Testing Proficiency**
APIs are critical—tools like Postman and knowledge of JSON/XML responses are must-haves.

### 3. **AI Integration**
AI tools like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) simplify processes such as defect prediction and generating test cases. Learning to leverage these will elevate your efficiency.

### 4. **Mobile and Browser Testing Expertise**
Master tools like BrowserStack for cross-platform testing on diverse devices and operating systems.

### 5. **Basic Coding**
SQL, Python, or JavaScript knowledge enables simple scripting for data validation or log analysis.

### 6. **Cybersecurity Insights**
Incorporate OWASP guidelines and develop penetration testing knowledge to strengthen application security.

---

Must-Have Soft Skills

### 1. **Attention to Detail**
Spotting nuances sets great testers apart. Stay curious and analytical to find hidden bugs others may overlook.

### 2. **Effective Communication**
From writing concise bug reports to collaborating with cross-functional teams, clear communication is essential.

### 3. **Customer-Centric Approach**
Testing with the end-user in mind ensures products not only work but delight the customers.

### 4. **Adaptability and Problem Solving**
QA requires thinking on your feet and continuous learning to address evolving challenges.
