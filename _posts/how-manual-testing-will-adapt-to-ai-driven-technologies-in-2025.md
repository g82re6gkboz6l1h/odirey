## The Role of Manual Testing in 2025

Manual testing's relevance lies in areas where AI falters, such as:

- **Defining Edge Cases:** Tackling rare real-world scenarios AI cannot predict.
- **Exploratory Testing:** Using creativity to find non-obvious bugs.
- **Usability Testing:** Evaluating human-centric factors like emotions and satisfaction.
- **Testing AI Systems:** Validating AI applications for ethics and accuracy.

Rather than being replaced, manual testers will work alongside AI, expanding their strategic role and leveraging technology to amplify their efforts.
