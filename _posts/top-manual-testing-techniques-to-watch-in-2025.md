Manual testing isn’t static—it’s becoming more agile, efficient, and inventive. These techniques will rule in 2025:

### **Risk-Based Manual Testing**
Focus manual testing efforts on high-risk areas where errors could harm user experience or functionality.

### **AI-Powered Exploratory Testing**
AI insights empower testers to identify where exploratory workflows yield rich findings, bridging human creativity with predictive intelligence.

### **Crowdsourced Testing for Diversity**
Harness global talent pools to uncover region-specific issues and enhance software adaptability.

### **Accessibility Testing as a Priority**
Manual testing ensures systems align with assistive technologies, boosting inclusivity and regulatory compliance.
