## Overview of Manual Testing and Automation in 2025

Manual testing involves human testers executing test scenarios without automation tools, ideal for usability, exploratory testing, and design flaw detection. Automated testing, on the other hand, utilizes advanced tools and scripts to perform repetitive, large-scale procedures, excelling in Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) pipelines. By 2025, AI-powered platforms like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) bolster test automation capabilities, ensuring faster and more comprehensive testing processes.

These approaches are complementary. Manual testing addresses complex scenarios with human creativity, while automation performs efficient regression tests, freeing teams to solve higher-order problems.
