Overcoming Challenges of AI Integration

AI tools generate immense value, but adoption barriers persist:
- Resistance from testers fearing job automation.
- Data bias impacting AI decisions.
- High deployment costs.

**Solutions:**
- Invest in upskilling testers to align human expertise with AI capabilities.
- Gradually phase in AI tools for smoother adaptivity.
- Reinforce that AI augments manual testing without replacing it.
- Communicate AI benefits transparently to build trust among teams.
