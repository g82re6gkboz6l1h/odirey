## Top Skills Manual Testers Must Hone for 2025

Manual testing continues to play a crucial role despite the automation shift. Here are key skills for manual testers:

1. **Critical Thinking**: Mastering analytical and creative thinking enables testers to identify nuanced edge cases automation might miss.

2. **Exploratory Testing Skills**: Human intuition and expertise make identifying defects in unconventional scenarios possible.

3. **Test Planning & Documentation**: Effective communication and precision in creating detailed test plans and cases are essential.

4. **Technical Know-How**: Familiarity with coding, APIs, SQL, and tools like Selenium and JIRA strengthens collaboration with developers.

5. **Adaptability to Emerging Trends**: Keep up with AI platforms like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) and CI/CD tools to optimize workflows and boost efficiency.
