## Critical Use Cases for Manual Testing in the AI Era
Some tasks resist full automation. Manual testing excels in areas requiring human ingenuity, including:
- **Exploratory Testing:** Unstructured and creative workflows.
- **Usability and UX Evaluation:** Subjective user experience insights.
- **Localized Testing:** Contextual verification for language or culture.
- **Complex Scenarios:** Edge cases beyond automation's scope.
- **Ethical Compliance:** Precise human judgment on fairness and bias.
