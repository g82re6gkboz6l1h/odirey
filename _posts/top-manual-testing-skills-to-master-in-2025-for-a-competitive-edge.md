## Top Skills for Manual Testers in 2025

### 1. **Automation Literacy**
   - Understand basics of programming languages like Python or JavaScript.
   - Utilize tools like Selenium, Cypress, and **Zof AI** to bridge manual-automation gaps.

### 2. **Critical Thinking**
   - Analyze complex scenarios and anticipate potential bugs to deliver reliable solutions.

### 3. **Effective Communication**
   - Facilitate collaboration with technical and non-technical teams through clear documentation and discussion.

### 4. **Adaptability**
   - Thrive in Agile/DevOps environments by reprioritizing effectively under tight timelines.

### 5. **Empathy for End-Users**
   - Anticipate real-world issues and edge cases, ensuring better usability and overall user satisfaction.

### 6. **Leveraging AI Tools**
   - Utilize platforms like **Zof AI** for AI-powered test case generation, defect prediction, and data reporting to improve testing efficiency.
