Discover the six must-have skills for manual testers to remain future-proof:

### 1. **Modern Software Architectures**
Master microservices, APIs, cloud platforms, and other complex architectures crucial for identifying fault points and enhancing system reliability.

### 2. **User Experience (UX) Testing**
Deepen your knowledge in usability, accessibility, and performing detailed UX evaluations to deliver seamless interactions.

### 3. **Advanced Test Case Design**
Sharpen your creativity to design and execute real-world scenario tests that automation may overlook.

### 5. **Collaboration Skills**

Invest in privacy compliance expertise and identify vulnerabilities, especially given rising cyber threats.
