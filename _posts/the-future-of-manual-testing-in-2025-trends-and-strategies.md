## Introduction to Manual Testing in 2025

Manual testing involves human testers actively interacting with software to identify bugs, validate functionalities, and ensure quality. This approach remains critical for: 

- **Exploratory testing**: Leveraging human intuition and creativity.
- **Usability and accessibility testing**: Ensuring user-friendly and inclusive designs.
- **Ad-hoc testing**: Handling unpredictable challenges beyond automation scripts.

Manual testing complements automation by handling nuanced, user-centered testing areas where human judgment excels.
