- Understand complex workflows and uncover edge cases beyond automation’s reach.
- Use data insights provided by AI tools to inject nuance into QA strategies.
- Enhance communication to act as a facilitator between developers, users, and stakeholders.
- Balance technical expertise with soft skills, like empathy and user-centered design thinking.

With advancements like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai), AI-powered tools are not replacing manual testing but complementing human effort to improve precision and efficiency, fundamentally altering the role of testers.
