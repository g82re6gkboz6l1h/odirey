Techniques for Perfecting Manual Testing

### 1. Understand Requirements
Analyze product requirements, designs, and user personas for better-targeted test cases.

### 2. Define Objectives
Focus tests on specific goals—performance, functionality, usability, etc.

### 3. Perform Smoke Testing
Early detection of major issues saves time before deeper testing begins.

### 4. Utilize Exploratory Testing
Discover usability issues and hidden bugs through a creative approach.

### 5. Document Steps
Thorough documentation simplifies reproduction of defects and developer collaboration.

### 6. Embrace Collaboration
Strong communication between testers and developers boosts efficiency and clarity.

Prioritizing Manual Test Scenarios

- **High-Risk Features**: Focus on critical areas prone to failure like authentication or payment gateways.
- **Core Business Impact**: Prioritize scenarios central to business functions.
- **Frequent User Features**: Test high-use features that affect user satisfaction.
- **Leverage Historical Data**: Identify hotspot issues from past releases.
- **Use AI Insights**: Tools like Zof AI help identify impactful test areas, enhancing efficiency.
