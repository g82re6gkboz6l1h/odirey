## Top Trends Shaping Manual Testing by 2025

### 1. **AI & Machine Learning in Manual Workflows**
AI tools assist manual testers by identifying potential defects, analyzing behaviors, and reducing redundancies. For example, Zof AI enables dynamic exploratory testing by predicting risk zones through historical data.

### 2. **Accessibility Testing Goes Mainstream**
Increased focus on inclusivity will make accessibility testing a priority. Stricter regulations globally will demand in-depth testing to meet standards, empowering manual testers to create equitable user experiences.

### 3. **Blockchain in QA Processes**
Blockchain technology will enhance data integrity and security validation. Manual testers will acquire critical knowledge of ledger mechanisms, verifying processes for tampering or anomalies.

### 4. **Hybrid Methodologies for Cross-Functional Teams**
Teams using agile, DevOps, and exploratory strategies will dominate workflows, supported by virtual collaboration tools like AR/VR environments.
