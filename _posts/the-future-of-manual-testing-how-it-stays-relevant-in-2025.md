## Emerging Technologies Impacting QA

Emerging technologies are reshaping QA processes, requiring manual testers to adapt:

### Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Automation
AI-powered tools, such as [Zof AI](https://zof.ai), streamline automated testing by reducing cycles and identifying defects with precision. Manual testers bring value by evaluating subjective areas, including UX, usability, and emotional aspects.

### Internet of Things (IoT)
IoT testing blends automation with manual techniques to evaluate performance, scalability, and unique real-world conditions that cannot be replicated by machines.

### AR/VR Testing
Immersive AR/VR applications demand manual testers to assess user experiences through real-world simulations, ensuring intuitive functionality.

### Blockchain Systems
Manual testing validates blockchain systems for security, compliance, and data authenticity, complementing the efficiency of automated frameworks.
