## What is Manual & Automation Testing?

**Manual Testing**: Human-driven, exploratory, and subjective testing focused on user experience, adaptability, and detecting non-quantifiable issues.

**Automation Testing**: Script-driven testing for repetitive tasks like regression, performance, and scalability checks, offering speed and consistency.

---

### Benefits of Manual Testing:
1. Identifies usability and design flaws.  
2. Excels in exploratory and edge-case scenarios.  
3. Provides deep feedback loops in initial development phases.  
4. Handles dynamic user workflows more effectively than automation.  

### Benefits of Automation Testing:
1. Handles repetitive and volume-driven tests efficiently.  
2. Enhances accuracy with minimal manual error.  
3. Scalable for performance and backend scenarios.
