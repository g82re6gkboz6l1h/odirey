## Manual Testing Strategies to Watch in 2025

1. **Blended Testing:** Hybrid frameworks combining manual and automated approaches.
2. **Shift-Left Testing:** Early involvement in the development lifecycle.
3. **Accessibility Testing:** Ensuring compliance with global accessibility standards.
4. **Behavior-Driven Development (BDD):** Writing user scenarios for feature alignment.
5. **Cross-Platform Testing:** Addressing challenges from IoT and diverse platforms.
