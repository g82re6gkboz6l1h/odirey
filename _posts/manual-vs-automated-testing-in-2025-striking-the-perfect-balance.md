## When Manual Testing Shines Over Automation
Prioritize manual testing when creativity and human insight are paramount:
1. **Exploratory Testing:** Discover hidden app vulnerabilities through intuitive and flexible investigations.
2. **UI/UX Validation:** Ensure visual and functional design meets emotional and cultural standards.
3. **Testing Edge Cases:** Assess unique scenarios like low battery performance or unconventional inputs.
4. **Early Development Bugs:** Quickly identify issues in the initial stages of development without stable scripts.
