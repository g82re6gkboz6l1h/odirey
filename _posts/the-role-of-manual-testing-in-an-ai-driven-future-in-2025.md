## Why Manual Testing Remains Vital in the AI Era
While AI excels at executing high-volume tasks, manual testers deliver key aspects that machines cannot replicate. Here’s why manual testing is essential:

### 1. User-Centric Analysis: 
Manual testers assess subjective user experiences—like software feel and accessibility—that AI struggles to evaluate.

### 2. Edge Case Validation:
Rare and unpredictable scenarios often require nuanced human judgment.

### 3. Adaptive Problem Solving:
Human testers excel in adjusting for evolving project requirements or detecting ambiguous data issues.

### 4. Exploratory Testing:
Unscripted exploration by testers reveals hidden bugs or usability flaws undetectable by algorithms.

### 5. Ethical Oversight:
Human experts ensure software aligns with ethical norms, preventing bias and adverse social impacts.
