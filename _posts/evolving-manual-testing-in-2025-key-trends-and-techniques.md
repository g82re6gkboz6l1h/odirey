## Techniques Revolutionizing Manual Testing in 2025

1. **Session-Based Test Management (SBTM)**: Focused, objective-driven testing sessions, supported by AI tools like Zof AI to optimize test coverage.
2. **Accessibility Testing**: Evaluating user accessibility for all individuals, including those with disabilities, ensures inclusive design.
3. **Usability Testing in Practical Scenarios**: Real-world simulation for user-friendly, practical software design insights.
4. **Combinatorial Testing**: Leveraging pairwise testing to uncover critical defects in complex software environments.
5. **Continuous Testing**: A key part of Agile/DevOps workflows, focusing on strategic reviews and change impact analysis.
6. **Cross-Browser and Compatibility Testing**: Ensuring consistent performance across devices, operating systems, and browsers.
7. **Test Case Optimization**: Emphasizing quality-driven, SMART test cases rather than excessive documentation.
