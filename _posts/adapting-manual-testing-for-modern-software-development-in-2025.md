With software development in 2025 advancing rapidly, incorporating AI, IoT, blockchain, and AR technology, manual testing must adapt to evolving complexities. As automation takes center stage, manual testing remains irreplaceable in addressing edge cases and human-centric scenarios. This article uncovers challenges, innovations, and strategies to future-proof manual testing processes.

The fast-paced software landscape demands delivery speed and uncompromised quality amid interconnected platforms and devices. Agile methodologies prioritize shorter development cycles, necessitating new approaches for manual testing to meet these demands.
