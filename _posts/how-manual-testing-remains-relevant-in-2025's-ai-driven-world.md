# The Relevance of Manual Testing in 2025’s AI-Driven QA Landscape

In today's AI-dominated world, quality assurance (QA) is undergoing a technological transformation. Automation powered by artificial intelligence (AI) brings speed, precision, and scalability. Yet manual testing, far from being phased out, has evolved to play a critical role in this ecosystem. This article explores manual testing’s indispensable role alongside AI technologies, such as [Zof AI](https://zof.ai), to achieve customer satisfaction and unparalleled quality assurance.
