#### Shifts in Manual Testing Methodologies

Manual testing, known for its unique human judgment and creativity, is adapting to stay effective. By 2025, manual testing will focus on:

- **Contextual Testing**: Real-world testing scenarios to enhance user experience and uncover issues automation can miss.
- **Critical Thinking & Innovation**: Going beyond predefined test cases to explore usability and intuitive design.
- **Holistic Software Quality**: Encompassing user aesthetics, accessibility, and empathy-driven evaluations, ensuring apps appeal to end-users.
