## **Why Manual Testing is Essential in the AI-Powered Future**
### **1. Exploratory Testing for Unknown Scenarios**  
When predefined scripts fail, manual testers shine by identifying unforeseen risks.

### **2. Usability and Accessibility Validation**  
Automation cannot replace human evaluation of UI/UX intricacies or accessibility standards.

### **3. Adaptability to Dynamic Requirements**  
Manual testers pivot quickly to address changing project goals, far beyond automation's rigid scripts.

### **4. Judgment-Driven Decision Making**  
Industries requiring ethical assessments and human judgment demand direct oversight.
