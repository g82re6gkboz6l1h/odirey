## Why Manual Testing Still Matters in 2025

1. **Human-Centric Insights**: Manual testing excels in scenarios that require emotional intelligence, such as accessibility audits and healthcare apps.
2. **Dynamic Adaption**: Agile and DevOps methodologies demand the flexibility of manual testing to address evolving requirements and unexpected changes.
3. **Edge Case Detection**: Unlike automation scripts, manual QA identifies rare bugs and unique scenarios that pre-programmed tools often miss.

Manual Testing vs. Automation: Advantages
- **Context Awareness**: QA engineers bring human intelligence to interpret nuanced scenarios.
- **Affordability for Small Projects**: Manual testing minimizes costs for startups and smaller-scale applications.
- **UI/UX Validation**: Design and usability feedback often require an in-depth, hands-on approach.
- **Adaptability in Unpredictability**: Manual testers can think critically and adapt, tackling issues automated tools can’t foresee.
