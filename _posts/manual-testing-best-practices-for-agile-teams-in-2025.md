## The Role of Manual Testing in Agile Frameworks

Agile projects focus on iterative development cycles called "sprints," demanding rapid deployment without sacrificing quality. Traditional testing methods often can't keep up, but manual testing fills critical gaps in exploratory testing, usability assessments, and identifying edge-case bugs.

### Why Manual Testing Is Crucial in Agile Projects
1. **Human-Centered Usability**: Automated systems validate functionality, but human intuition resolves usability issues and real-world expectations.
2. **Agility**: Manual testers pivot quickly in response to evolving priorities, addressing risks in real-time.
3. **Automation Complement**: While automation excels in bulk tasks, manual testing is unmatched for exploring edge scenarios and dynamic requirements.

### Key Challenges for Manual Testers:
- Balancing quality under time pressure in fast-moving Agile work.
- Ensuring synergy between testers, developers, and product managers.
- Prioritizing testing efforts for comprehensive coverage despite tight deadlines.

- Ensuring critical features work as expected before deployment.

### Best Practices:
1. Combine automated regression tests with manual edge-case testing in parallel.
