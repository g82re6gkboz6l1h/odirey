## Challenges Facing Manual Testing by 2025

### 1. Scaling Across Multiple Platforms
Modern software needs to function perfectly across various devices, operating systems, and configurations, leading to rising complexity for testers.

### 2. Managing Interconnected Systems
Systems today rely on APIs, microservices, and third-party integrations, requiring intricate manual testing strategies.

### 3. Keeping Pace with Faster Development
Agile and CI/CD practices demand efficient yet deep testing within significantly shorter timelines.

### 4. Ensuring Security and Compliance
Manual testers must ensure that software complies with data privacy and security regulations without sacrificing thoroughness.

These challenges highlight why testers must adapt using AI-powered solutions while maintaining manual testing’s core value: the human-driven perspective on user experience.
