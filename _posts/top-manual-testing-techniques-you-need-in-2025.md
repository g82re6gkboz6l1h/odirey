In 2025, usability testing remains vital in creating user-friendly products. Manual assessments ensure a stronger emotional connection through human creativity and empathy, addressing user frustrations effectively.

AI-powered tools such as Zof AI help predict usability bottlenecks and streamline data collected during test sessions.

### **4. Cross-Platform Testing**
Cross-browser and cross-platform testing are indispensable as users expect seamless experiences across devices and browsers. Manual testers can detect visual inconsistencies and subtle interactions missed by automation.

### **5. Ad-hoc Testing**
The randomness of ad-hoc testing proves valuable in discovering unforeseen bugs without formal structures or test plans. While unplanned, coupling this method with analytics platforms enhances its effectiveness for emerging tech like IoT.
