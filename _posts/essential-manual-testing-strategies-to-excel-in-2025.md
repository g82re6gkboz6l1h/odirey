## Strategies for Success in Agile and DevOps Testing

Adapt to faster release cycles with these proven tactics:

1. **Exploratory Testing**: Discover hidden bugs through creative and intuitive navigation.
2. **Shift-left Testing**: Identify issues early to reduce risks and costs.
3. **Collaboration with Automation Engineers**: Combine forces to improve efficiency.
4. **Risk-based Testing**: Prioritize critical components with targeted efforts.
5. **Leverage AI-driven Platforms**: Use tools like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) for predictive insights.
