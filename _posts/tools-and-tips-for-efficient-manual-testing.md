Crafting Winning Test Cases for Manual Testing
Turn your test cases into actionable and insightful tools:

### 1. **Establish Objectives**
Clear objectives make testing focused and impactful—be it functional, regression, or usability testing.

### 2. **Detail Each Step**
Create instructions that are simple, clear, with actionable checkpoints.

### 3. **Use Descriptive Titles**
Example: Replace "Check login" with "Verify login functionality with valid credentials."

### 4. **Note Preconditions**
Clearly define dependencies or required prerequisites.

### 5. **Prioritize Strategically**
Rank test cases as high, medium, or low priority—ensuring critical areas are completed first.

### 6. **Adopt Reusable Scenarios**
Reusable frameworks save time and enhance consistency.

### 3. **Document Findings**
Take meticulous notes, screenshots, or recordings for traceability.

### 4. **Use Session-Based Testing**
