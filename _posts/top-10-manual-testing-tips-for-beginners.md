## **What is Manual Testing for QA Beginners?**

Manual testing involves assessing software without automation tools. It lets QA engineers manually execute test cases to ensure functionality, design, and performance align with user expectations. Here's why it's vital:

### Tasks as a Manual Tester:
- Analyzing system requirements.
- Creating and executing test cases.
- Detecting bugs and documenting thoroughly.
- Collaborating with development teams to resolve issues.

As a beginner, you'll face challenges like repetitive testing and balancing precision with speed. Utilizing tools like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) can help overcome these obstacles by optimizing documentation and organization.
