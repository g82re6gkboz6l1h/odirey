## Why Manual Testing Remains Crucial

In a rapidly evolving tech-driven world, manual testing continues to play a key role in delivering high-quality software. While automation testing dominates in certain areas, manual testing remains vital for detecting issues that rely on human intuition, exploratory investigation, and user-centric analysis. This guide explores the top manual testing techniques you need to master to thrive as a software tester in 2025.

---

### 1. Core Principles of Manual Testing

Understanding the basics of manual testing is the foundation of success. Manual testing allows testers to step into the shoes of end-users to evaluate features, usability, and application behavior.

#### Key Concepts:
- Test Case Creation: Crafting detailed test cases for standard and edge scenarios.
- Exploratory Testing: Identifying hidden bugs through unstructured user interactions.
- Bug Logging & Tracking: Properly documenting and tracking bug fixes.

---

### 2. Advanced Manual Testing Techniques for 2025

#### a) Session-Based Test Management (SBTM):
Organize tasks into structured, objective sessions to maximize efficiency.

#### b) Cross-Browser & Device Testing:
Manually validate applications across multiple platforms and devices to uncover critical usability issues.

#### c) Risk-Based Testing Techniques:
Focus manual effort on high-risk areas using sophisticated, data-driven risk assessments.
