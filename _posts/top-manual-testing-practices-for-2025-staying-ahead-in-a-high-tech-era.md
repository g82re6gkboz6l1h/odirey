## Manual Testing Best Practices for 2025

1. **Document Test Plans:** Define objectives, timelines, scope, and deliverables succinctly.
2. **Cross-Platform Testing:** Cover mobile, wearable, IoT, and desktop platforms.
3. **Human-Centric Validation:** Prioritize real-world use cases and subjective UX issues.
4. **Detailed Bug Reports:** Use logs or screenshots to improve developer collaboration.
5. **Ongoing Team Collaboration:** Foster strong communication with regular brainstorming sessions.
