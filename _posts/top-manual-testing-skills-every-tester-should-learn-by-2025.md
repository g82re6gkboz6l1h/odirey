## Why Manual Testing Skills Are Irreplaceable

Automation testing has made vast strides, yet manual testing provides critical human-centric benefits:

1. **User Perspective Analysis:** Manual testers assess software intuitively with empathy for users, spotting nuanced design flaws.
2. **Edge Case Identification:** Humans excel in testing unforeseen scenarios and handling exceptions automated scripts miss.
3. **Exploratory Testing Creativity:** Only manual testers adapt and probe systems innovatively in real-time.
4. **Validating Automation:** Manual testers ensure AI or automation tools produce accurate results, bridging human and AI analysis effectively.
