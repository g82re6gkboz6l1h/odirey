## Why Manual Testing Matters in an AI-Driven World
Manual testing remains relevant due to its unique ability to address subjective dimensions of software, such as user behavior, accessibility, and aesthetics. Unlike automated systems, human testers bring intuition and empathy into the process, ensuring applications resonate with end-users. By complementing automation, manual testers deliver comprehensive quality assurance in hybrid ecosystems dominated by DevOps and Agile methodologies.
