## Challenges Manual Testers Will Face in 2025

### Increasing Complexity in Applications
With IoT integrations, machine learning models, and cloud technologies, application functionalities extend to usability and ethical concerns like unbiased AI outputs.

### Demand for Faster Delivery with Continuous Deployment
Testers face pressure from fast-paced DevOps workflows. Manual testing must be optimized for speed without compromising thoroughness.

### Enhanced Security Testing Requirements
The rise in cyber threats demands robust manual testing for compliance with GDPR, HIPAA, and other evolving regulations.

### AI Testing Challenges
Testing AI systems and ensuring fair results across diverse demographics will require a new level of expertise from manual testers.
