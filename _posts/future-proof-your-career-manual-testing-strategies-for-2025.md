Testing methodologies are evolving alongside tech innovations. This section explores the interplay between automation, AI tools, and manual expertise while preparing for a landscape where real-world issues will demand human ingenuity.

2. **Emerging Technologies**: Technologies like AR/VR, blockchain, and quantum computing require manual testers to upskill and approach challenges creatively.
3. **Manual Expertise Importance**: The human touch provides a unique layer to ensuring quality that AI alone cannot offer.
