## Manual Testing vs Automated Testing: Key Differences in 2025

Automation, enhanced by AI, dominates repetitive tasks like regression testing and performance benchmarks. However, manual testing continues to excel where human insight and interaction are essential:

- **Exploratory Testing:** Unearthing subtle edge cases.
- **Usability Testing:** Evaluating user experience based on behavioral insights.
- **Ad hoc Testing:** Adapting to dynamic, real-time challenges.
