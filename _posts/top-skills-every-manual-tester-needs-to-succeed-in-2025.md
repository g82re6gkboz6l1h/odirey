## Top Skills Manual Testers Need to Develop

### Critical Soft Skills: Thinking and Adaptability
Manual testers must focus on critical thinking and adaptability to navigate challenges effectively:

- **Critical Thinking**: Testers should analyze software usability, predict potential risks, and proactively uncover edge cases automation may miss.
- **Adaptability**: The ability to learn and adjust to new tools, workflows, and collaborations is key to staying ahead in a dynamic industry.

### AI Integration and Technical Knowledge
With the rise of AI-driven testing tools, manual testers should:

- **Understand AI-Driven Tools**: Familiarity with platforms like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) empowers testers to complement automation with manual insights.
- **Data Analytics**: Testers must harness testing data to assess risks, log analysis, and improve decisions.
- **API Testing**: API testing is a crucial skill for evaluating back-end system interactions and ensuring microservices efficiency.
