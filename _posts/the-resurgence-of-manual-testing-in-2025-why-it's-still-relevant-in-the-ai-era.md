## What is Manual Testing in the Age of AI?

Though perceived as traditional, manual testing excels at evaluating user behavior, detecting edge cases, and ensuring usability. Automated testing might streamline repetitive tasks, but human intuition and adaptability remain irreplaceable for uniquely challenging scenarios. In industries like healthcare, finance, and eCommerce, the high stakes demand both automation and the human touch.

## The Case for Keeping Manual Testing Alive

Automated testing tools—like predictive algorithms and machine learning frameworks—are more powerful than ever. Yet AI has limitations because:

- **It can’t analyze human emotions** or understand nuanced UX issues.
- **It struggles with edge cases**, infrequent scenarios only humans may predict and resolve.
- **Exploratory testing**, requiring creativity, can’t be scripted efficiently.
- **AI outputs require human validation** to identify biases and ensure consistent accuracy.
