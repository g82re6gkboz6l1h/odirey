Balancing Automation and Manual Testing

While automation offers speed and scalability, manual testing retains its value, particularly for exploratory and usability scenarios. Striking a balance between these approaches is the key to optimal QA outcomes.

### When to Automate:
- **Regression Testing:** Automating routine regression tests reduces manual workload.
- **Performance Testing:** AI tools like Zof AI excel at executing large-scale performance benchmarks.
- **Data-Intensive Tests:** Automation handles repetitive test execution more effectively.

### When Manual Testing is Essential:
- **Exploratory Testing:** Creativity and intuition are best employed by human testers.
- **UX and Usability Evaluation:** Human judgment is critical for user experience-based insights.
- **Ambiguous Scenarios:** Complex or unclear requirements are better analyzed manually.

The collaboration between AI tools and manual testing ensures comprehensive QA while optimizing resource allocation.
