## Why Manual Testing Matters for AI Applications

AI systems function differently from traditional software due to their reliance on machine learning, complex algorithms, and dynamic data-driven decisions. Manual testing ensures excellence in areas automated tools may overlook:

- **Ethical Compliance**: Verifying regulatory adherence.
- **User Experience**: Evaluating usability and accessibility.
- **Edge Cases**: Detecting uncommon or unusual issues.
- **Bias Identification**: Identifying biases within AI models or datasets.

As AI systems evolve, manual testing ensures these solutions meet ethical and operational standards critical to 2025's technosphere.
