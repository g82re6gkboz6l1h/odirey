- **Hyper-Personalized Testing Analytics**: Ensure applications cater to diverse user needs effectively.

- **Accessibility Testing**: Promote inclusivity and compliance with standards like WCAG.
- **AI Collaboration**: Leverage AI-powered tools for smarter defect tracking and exploratory testing.
