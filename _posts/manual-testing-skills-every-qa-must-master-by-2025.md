## Key Manual Testing Competencies for 2025
Ensure your QA career stays relevant with these skills:

1. **Critical Thinking:** Discern design inconsistencies and potential failures.
2. **Domain Expertise:** Gain knowledge in industry-specific standards like HIPAA or PCI compliance.
3. **UX/UI Testing Mastery:** Sharpen your design and accessibility analysis skills.
4. **Communication Skills:** Provide actionable bug reports and coordinate with teams effectively.
5. **Automation Knowledge:** Familiarize with tools like Selenium for hybrid workflows.
