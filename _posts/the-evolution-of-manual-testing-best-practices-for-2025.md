## Why Manual Testing Remains Essential in 2025

Even as automated testing revolutionizes quality assurance, manual testing addresses critical areas that automation cannot fully replicate:

1. **Human-Centric Testing**: Captures usability nuances and detects visual inconsistencies.
2. **Exploratory Testing**: Enables testers to probe unexpected bugs and system behavior.
3. **Accessibility Validation**: Assesses the end-user experience for accessibility compliance.
4. **Short-Term Projects**: Offers cost-efficiency when automation feels excessive for shorter lifecycles.
5. **Dynamic Platforms**: Complements automation for complex, fast-deploying systems.
