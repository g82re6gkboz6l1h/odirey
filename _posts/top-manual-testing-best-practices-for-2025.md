## Why are Manual Testing Practices Evolving in 2025?

The software development industry is progressing rapidly, driven by new technologies and growing customer expectations. Agile, DevOps, and innovations like AI are reshaping workflows, making it essential to adapt how manual testing is approached.

Key drivers for updated manual testing practices in 2025 include:

1. **Complex Software Architectures**: Modern applications span platforms like mobile, desktop, IoT, making multi-dimensional testing critical.
2. **AI Evolution**: Automation of mundane tasks and better decision-making through AI.
3. **Focus on User Experience**: High emphasis on security, performance, and user satisfaction.
4. **Collaborative Ecosystem**: The need for automation and manual testing to coexist effectively.

By incorporating cutting-edge tools and strategies, testers can maintain relevance and tackle challenges in this advanced environment.

2. **Focus on High-Stakes Areas**: Utilize AI tools (e.g., Zof AI) to identify what areas require the most attention.
