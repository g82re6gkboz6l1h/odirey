## Why Manual Testing Thrives in 2025 

Automation is powerful, but manual testing offers unique advantages like intuition, creativity, and empathy that machines can't replicate. From user experience validation and exploratory testing to handling complex dynamic scenarios, manual testing brings human-centric insights to software quality assurance. Additionally, for startups or small-scale projects, manual testing often provides a cost-effective alternative to automation.

Time-Saving Techniques for Manual Testers 

Staying productive in manual testing requires smart strategies:  
1. **Prioritize Test Scenarios**: Focus testing on high-risk and high-impact areas.  
2. **Session-Based Test Management (SBTM)**: Structured sessions drive efficiency in exploratory testing.  
3. **Pair Testing**: Two testers working together enhance precision and coverage.  
4. **Checklists**: Ensure no critical step is overlooked in complex workflows.  
5. **Rapid Reporting**: Opt for concise bug reports with clear replication steps.
