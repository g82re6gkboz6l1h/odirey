## Key Manual Testing Strategies for 2025

### **1. Understand Stakeholder and User Goals**
Align testing efforts with business objectives and user needs to prioritize essential workflows.

### **2. Comprehensive Test Case Creation**
Focus on edge cases, usability checks, and well-documented scenarios for streamlined bug replication.

### **3. Exploratory Testing Adoption**
Simulate real-world use cases with creative, unscripted interactions to uncover latent issues.

### **4. Lean on Risk-Based Testing**
Prioritize critical components and business-sensitive areas to maximize testing efficiency.

### **5. Agile Workflow Integration**
Adopt short, iterative cycles and ensure regression tests occur alongside every update.
