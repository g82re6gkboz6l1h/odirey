   With fast product cycles, manual testing integrates into CI/CD pipelines for iterative quality assurance throughout development phases.

2. **The Rise of AI and Automation**  
   AI-powered tools like Zof AI are revolutionizing testing processes by reducing repetitive tasks and increasing bug detection efficiency, while maintaining human oversight for usability testing.
