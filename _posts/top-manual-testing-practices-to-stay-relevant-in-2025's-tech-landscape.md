## Navigating Emerging Technologies in Testing

The technological landscape of 2025 incorporates:

1. **AI-Powered Applications:** Manual testing addresses AI unpredictability through ethical and usability assessments.
2. **Blockchain Systems:** This requires thorough verifications of smart contracts and transaction security.
3. **IoT Devices:** Testers ensure connection stability and evaluate environmental interactions.
4. **Quantum Computing:** The unique algorithms of quantum technology require innovative manual testing approaches.

To thrive, testers must combine manual expertise with automation and adopt a hybrid approach to testing.
