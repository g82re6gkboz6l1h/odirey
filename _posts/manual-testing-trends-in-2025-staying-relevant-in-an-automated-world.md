1. **Exploratory Testing**: Address unknown factors and hidden defects beyond the scope of automation.
2. **Usability Assessments**: Empathize with users and analyze unpredictable behaviors.
3. **Complex Scenarios**: Evaluate intricate multi-variable applications.
4. **Physical Environment Testing**: Examine hardware-integrated apps under varying conditions.
