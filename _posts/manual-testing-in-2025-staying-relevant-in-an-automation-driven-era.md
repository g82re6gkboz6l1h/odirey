2. **Exploratory Testing**: Manual testers uncover hard-to-predict flaws automation often misses.
3. **Dynamic Flexibility in Agile Development**: Manual testing adapts quicker to sudden changes in iterative environments.

## Finding the Right Automation and Manual Testing Balance

Testing professionals in 2025 must strategically integrate automation with manual processes for comprehensive results. Here's how they complement each other:

### Automation Excels:
- Automates regression, performance, and load testing.
- Reduces repetitive tasks, boosting overall efficiency.

### Manual Testing Excels:
- Investigates user-centric aspects like UX validation and accessibility.
- Tackles ambiguous scenarios that require human decision-making.
