Software testing is undergoing a major transformation as automation continues to redefine how systems are built, tested, and deployed. However, even as we venture into 2025, **manual testing** remains a critical component of the quality assurance (QA) process. This article explores why the synergy between manual testing and automation is becoming more relevant than ever. 

Discover the unique advantages of manual testing, innovative tools like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai), and emerging methodologies to keep manual testing vital in 2025's fast-paced development landscape.
