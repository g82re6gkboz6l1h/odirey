## Key Challenges Faced by Manual Testers in 2025

1. **Increased Testing Accuracy Demands**: User expectations for flawless functionality and seamless interfaces require extensive test coverage, balancing empathy with efficiency.
2. **Time Constraints**: Agile-driven cycles leave testers vulnerable to rushing processes and overlooking complexities.
3. **Human Error Risks**: Fatigue and errors jeopardize test reliability.
4. **Limited Adaptability for Large Systems**: Diverse functionalities strain traditional manual testing methods.
5. **Collaboration Bottlenecks**: Inefficiencies arise from disconnected teams.
