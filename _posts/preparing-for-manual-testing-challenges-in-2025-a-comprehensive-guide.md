Modern Manual Testing Methodologies

Adaptability will be crucial for testers in 2025. Embrace strategies like:

- **Shift-Left Testing:** Proactively identifying issues during earlier development phases.
- **Behavior-Driven Testing (BDD):** Focusing on user-centric scenarios.
- **Hybrid Testing Models:** Utilizing automation for repetitive backend tasks while focusing manual efforts on exploratory and UX testing.
- **Continuous Feedback Loops:** Refining testing processes through user feedback.
