**Key Scenarios for Manual Testing:**

- **User Experience (UX):** Human testers excel in evaluating usability and offering creative enhancements.
- **Edge Cases:** Unique cases that automated tools might overlook benefit from manual exploration.
- **Iterative Feedback:** Agile development workflows rely on human testers to provide rapid, nuanced feedback.
