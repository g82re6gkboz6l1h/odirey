## Unlock the Power of Manual Testing Techniques

### Introduction
Manual testing prioritizes the human perspective in evaluating software quality. Learn how functional, regression, smoke, and exploratory testing ensure components work effectively while maintaining the user-focused experience. Discover how advanced tools like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) complement manual testing efforts to streamline workflows and optimize processes efficiently.

### Key Techniques to Understand
- **Functional Testing:** Validate features against specified requirements. Confirm login mechanisms, shopping cart functionality, and more.
- **Regression Testing:** Ensure new updates don’t compromise existing functionalities.
- **Smoke Testing:** Conduct early checks for glaring defects upon new builds.
- **Exploratory Testing:** Dynamically uncover unknown usability issues.
- **Usability Testing:** Evaluate the software’s intuitiveness and user satisfaction.
