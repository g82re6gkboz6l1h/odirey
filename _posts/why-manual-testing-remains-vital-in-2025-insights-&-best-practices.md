## Why Manual Testing Still Matters

Many believe automation has fully replaced manual testing, but this overlooks key scenarios only humans can address. Here’s why manual testing remains critical:

### 1. **Tackling Unpredictable Scenarios**
Real user behavior is often erratic and non-linear. Human testers can employ creativity to simulate unpredictable use cases that automation tools cannot foresee.

### 2. **Spotting Design Flaws**
Automation struggles to detect subtle UX or UI issues, like confusing layouts or emotionally disruptive design elements, that humans can identify instantly.

### 3. **Testing Early-Stage Features**
For new features lacking test scripts, manual testing offers rapid feedback, fostering communication between development and QA teams to refine the product.
