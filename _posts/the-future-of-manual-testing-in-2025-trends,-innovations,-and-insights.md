### Top Trends Reshaping Manual Testing in 2025
- Increased focus on UX and accessibility.
- Rise of exploratory testing to identify critical bugs.
- Collaboration between automation and manual testing.
- Boost in cross-disciplinary testing skills, including API and security.
- Data-driven testing using predictive tools and analytics.

### The Value of Manual Testing
Human intuition, exploratory testing, and empathy remain irreplaceable in software QA, ensuring usability and cost-effectiveness for organizations trying to balance automated and manual efforts.
