## The Relevance of Manual Testing in 2025

Automation tests may dominate repetitive and predictable software scenarios, but manual testing thrives in nuanced areas like usability, exploratory assessments, and user experience. These areas require human intervention to ensure products align with end-user expectations. As we approach 2025 and beyond, the landscape of manual testing will adopt innovative methodologies that blend human creativity and AI technology. Instead of replacing humans, AI tools will complement their efforts, allowing testers to focus on complex issue identification and customer satisfaction.
