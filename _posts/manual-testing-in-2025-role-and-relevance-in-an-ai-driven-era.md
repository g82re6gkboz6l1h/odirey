# The Relevance of Manual Testing in 2025: Adapting to an AI-Driven Landscape

Even as advancements in AI dominate the tech sector, manual testing retains its edge by providing flexibility, creativity, and intuition in dynamic testing environments. Manual testers face challenges as the AI revolution reshapes quality assurance, such as:
