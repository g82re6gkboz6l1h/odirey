3. The Rise of User-Experience-Centric Testing

User-experience (UX) testing is becoming a central strategy for companies aiming to create emotionally engaging, user-friendly products. Manual testers simulate real-world scenarios, evaluate usability, and ensure the software meets user expectations. 4. Hybrid Testing: Integrating Manual and Automated Pipelines

By 2025, hybrid testing approaches combining manual and automated methods will dominate. While automation handles large-scale repetitive tasks, manual testing excels in exploring nuanced functionality and usability. Integrated pipelines allow automation to detect patterns for deeper human investigation, enhancing speed, collaboration, and software quality.
