## Evolving Methodologies: Blending Manual and Automated Testing

Automation shines for routine tasks, but manual testing thrives in areas requiring intuition and creativity. Strategies like exploratory testing, shift-left integration, and continuous testing redefine QA practices, fostering synergy between human expertise and AI-driven automation.

### Methodological Highlights:
1. **Exploratory Techniques**: Creative testing pathways uncover hidden issues aided by AI insights.
2. **Shift-Left Testing**: Early testing engagement to identify problems before they escalate.
3. **Iterative Assessments**: Real-time manual assurance during frequent software updates.
