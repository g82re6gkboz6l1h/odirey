## Key Challenges Facing Manual Testing
Manual testing has always been a pillar of QA, but emerging trends and technological advancements bring vital challenges:

1. **Increased Software Complexity:** Applications now involve APIs, microservices, and diverse cloud ecosystems, demanding high proficiency in manual QA over intricate workflows.
2. **Shorter Development Timelines:** Agile methodologies have drastically reduced testing durations, compelling testers to deliver results faster.
3. **AI & Automation Integration:** Manual roles face ambiguity with increasing automation.
4. **Pressure for Results:** Organizations crave efficient processes. Manual QA needs visible differentiation from AI-led automation processes.
