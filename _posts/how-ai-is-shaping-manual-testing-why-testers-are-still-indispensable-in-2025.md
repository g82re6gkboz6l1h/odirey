## **Why Manual Testers Are Crucial in 2025**

Despite the advancements in AI, human testers deliver unique value:

### 1. **Human-Centric Expertise**
Manual testers assess user experiences and gauge software intuitiveness—areas where AI falls short.

### 2. **Exploratory Testing**
Explorative testing strategies rely on creativity and knowledge, critical for uncovering hidden issues.

### 3. **Domain-Specific Insights**
Specialized sectors like healthcare demand human testers to navigate complex workflows and regulations.

### 4. **AI Oversight**
AI outputs often require validation, with manual testers ensuring accuracy and relevance.
