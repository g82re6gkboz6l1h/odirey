- **Faster Delivery Needs**: QA teams must maintain quality while adhering to tight Agile and DevOps timelines.

- **Skill Integration**: Manual testers may struggle to adapt to AI-powered tools.

Zof AI offers solutions to these concerns by reducing manual effort, automating repetitive actions, and enhancing human skills with actionable insights.
