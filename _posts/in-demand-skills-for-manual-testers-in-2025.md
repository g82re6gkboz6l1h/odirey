## Why Manual Testing is Crucial in 2025

### Human-Centric Testing Approach
Manual testers offer unique perspectives that prioritize user experience, accessibility, and real-world application behavior.

### Exploring Creative Scenarios
Automation tools tackle repetition, but manual testers excel in exploratory testing, finding edge cases and unexpected bugs.

### Cost-Effective Solutions for Dynamic Projects
For smaller projects or constantly changing requirements, manual testing remains a practical option.

### Complementary to Automation & AI
Manual testers collaborate with automation platforms for seamless execution and improved test strategies.
