## Manual Testing in 2025: Trends and Expectations

Although automation and AI dominate conversations in QA, manual testing excels where human insight is irreplaceable:

- **Exploratory Testing:** Identifying unforeseen application flaws using creativity and intuition.
- **User Experience (UX) Insights:** Evaluating app usability, aesthetics, and overall emotional impact.
- **Edge Case Resolution:** Uncovering unique user scenarios often overlooked by automated tests.

Shifting to 2025, manual QA engineers will bridge the gap between technology-driven workflows and critical user-centric outcomes.
