## Leading Manual Testing Strategies for Modern Software

### 1. **Exploratory Testing**
Exploratory testing will remain critical for uncovering unique issues in dynamic systems. This creative, hands-on technique excels in agile and AI-driven environments.

### 2. **Risk-Based Testing**
Focusing on high-risk areas ensures efficient allocation of testing efforts, particularly for distributed and security-critical applications.

### 3. **Usability Testing**
The manual touch is vital for evaluating user-friendliness, identifying friction points in workflows, and ensuring inclusivity in AI applications.

### 4. **Regression Validation**
Manual audits of edge cases bolster trust in CI/CD pipelines, confirming no existing functionalities are impacted by updates.

### 5. **Adhoc Testing**
Fast-paced domains benefit from the immediacy of adhoc testing, which excels in detecting high-priority bugs under constrained timelines.
