Manual Testing Techniques for QA Team Success

To maximize tool efficiency, QA teams must master these **essential testing techniques**:

1. **Exploratory Testing**: Leverage testers’ domain expertise to creatively uncover inconsistencies undetected by automation.
2. **Boundary Value Testing**: Assess behavior at application limits to identify extreme-case vulnerabilities.
3. **Usability Testing**: Simulate user journeys to ensure seamless user satisfaction and interface performance.
4. **Error Guessing**: Utilize tester intuition to identify recurring bug patterns efficiently.
5. **Checklist-Based Testing**: Follow predefined benchmarks to ensure comprehensive feature functionality reviews.
6. **Regression Testing**: Manually validate critical areas post-code modifications for unmatched reliability.
