## Why Manual and Automation Testing Are Equally Important

Manual testing relies on the human touch, critical for exploratory and usability testing, where emotional and contextual evaluations are paramount. Scenarios like accessibility checks or design flaws detection are uniquely suited to manual oversight.

Conversely, automated testing ensures consistency and speed for repetitive tasks like regression, load, and performance testing. Scalability in CI/CD pipelines necessitates automation for fast iterations. However, neglecting manual testing risks missing nuanced errors.
