## Leading Manual Testing Strategies for 2025

### **Exploratory Testing**
Exploratory testing enables testers to discover defects outside predefined test cases, leveraging domain knowledge and creativity. Essential for agile development, it ensures apps meet end-user expectations.

### **Risk-Based Testing**
Prioritize high-risk areas for efficient testing. Vital for industries like healthcare and finance, risk-based testing focuses efforts on critical components to enhance fault tolerance.

### **Usability Testing**
Assess UI, workflows, and accessibility for intuitive user experiences. As applications cater to global audiences, usability testing emphasizes inclusivity and accessibility for diverse needs.

### **Ad Hoc Testing**
Uncover errors through spontaneous, unstructured testing. Though informal, ad hoc testing detects overlooked defects beyond systematic approaches.

### **Regression Testing**
Manual regression testing complements automation to validate UI and edge cases missed by automated scripts, ensuring software enhancements do not disrupt existing functionality.

### **End-to-End Testing**
Manual E2E testing verifies complete workflows across connected systems and integrations, ensuring consistency within cloud-native and multi-layered architectures.
