# The Evolution of Manual Testing in 2025: Best Practices for Modern QA Teams

Manual testing remains irreplaceable for evaluating usability, exploratory scenarios, and human-centered experiences, complementing automated approaches. Modern QA professionals focus on accessibility, ethical AI validation, and emotional user experiences, reflecting the expanded scope of their discipline.
