Despite the rise of AI, human oversight in software testing ensures empathy, ethical judgment, and an understanding of **user experience (UX)**—a dimension technology cannot replicate. Key areas where manual testers thrive include:

- **Exploratory Testing**: Creativity and adaptability uncover unforeseen bugs.
- **User Experience Validation**: Humans evaluate software intuitively for accessibility and satisfaction.
- **Mitigating False Positives**: Manual intervention addresses ambiguities AI might misjudge.
- **Ethics in QA**: Decisions around sensitive user data remain uniquely human.
