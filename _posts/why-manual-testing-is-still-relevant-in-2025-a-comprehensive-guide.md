## Debunking the Myth: Manual Testing vs Automation

Contrary to popular belief, automation testing cannot fully replace manual testing. Automation excels in repetitive and predefined scenarios but lacks the intuition and creativity necessary to evaluate usability and design cohesion. Manual testers bring:

1. **Contextual Understanding**: Evaluating whether features align intuitively with user expectations.
2. **Exploratory Testing**: Discovering edge cases and unexpected issues, which automation often overlooks.
3. **User-Centric Feedback**: Assessing emotional responses and overall user satisfaction.

Both methodologies serve distinct yet complementary roles in QA.
