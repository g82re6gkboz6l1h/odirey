## Introduction to Manual Testing in 2025
Manual testing ensures software functions correctly from a human perspective. Historically centered around exploratory testing, usability checks, and UI validation, manual testing now complements automated workflows. With the rise of complex platforms and AI-powered solutions, manual testing accentuates areas requiring human judgment, like empathy-driven use cases and real-time adaptability.
