## 1. Key Challenges in Manual Testing

### Common Issues:
- **Human Error**: Repetitive tasks increase the likelihood of mistakes.
- **Time Constraints**: Manual tests take longer compared to automated processes.
- **Complex Scenarios**: Modern applications demand in-depth testing.
- **Lack of Standardized Documentation**: Variations across testers compromise test accuracy.
- **Resource Intensive**: Manual testing often requires significant effort, time, and skills.
- **Repetition Problems**: Regression testing is labor-intensive.

These hurdles emphasize the need for structured solutions and efficient workflows.
