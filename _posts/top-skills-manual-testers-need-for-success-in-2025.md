## Top Skills Manual Testers Need in 2025

### 1. **Analytical Thinking**
Thorough analytical thinking helps testers identify risks, decode patterns, and uncover hidden usability hurdles. In 2025, analytical thinking allows manual testers to:

- Spot issues automation might miss.
- Assess application performance and user experience.

With increasingly complex systems, this skill ensures excellent product reliability and quality.

### 2. **Adaptability**
Manual testers must thrive amid constant tech changes. Adaptability ensures testers:

- Handle continuous integration pipelines.
- Keep pace with Agile and DevOps methodologies.

Adaptable testers with a growth mindset remain valuable assets, especially as manual workflows integrate advanced AI systems.
