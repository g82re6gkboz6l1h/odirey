## 5 Essential Manual Testing Techniques Every Tester Must Know

### 1. **Exploratory Testing**
Go beyond scripts—explore software dynamically. Discover unforeseen bugs with creativity and expertise while refining session documentation for efficient replication.

### 2. **Regression Testing**
Ensure that software updates don’t harm existing functionalities. Humans excel with visuals, where bounce-back errors often hide beyond automation's scope.

### 3. **Usability Testing**
Evaluate what automation can’t! From ease of navigation to intuitive designs, boost UX standards via usability studies.

### 4. **Ad Hoc Testing**
This non-scripted approach provides flexibility and reveals unpredictable issues, complementing exploratory activities.

### 5. **Integration Testing**
Manually verify interdependencies between modules, catching potential failures early in project life cycles.
