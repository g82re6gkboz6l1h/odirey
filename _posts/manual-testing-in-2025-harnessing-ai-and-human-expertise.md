## How AI Enhances Manual Testing

AI is revolutionizing manual testing by automating repetitive tasks, analyzing data patterns, predicting risky areas, and even suggesting test cases. Key benefits include:

1. **Test Prioritization:** AI tools identify test cases that require more attention, ensuring testers focus on high-risk areas.
2. **Smart Suggestions:** AI improves test coverage by proposing edge-case scenarios, reducing oversight.
3. **Bug Prediction:** By analyzing code changes and history, AI predicts potential problem areas for targeted testing.
4. **Global Collaboration:** Tools like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) enable teams worldwide to share insights and best practices through data-driven analysis.
5. **Exploratory Testing Insights:** AI provides real-time metrics and guidance that enhance exploratory testing efforts.
