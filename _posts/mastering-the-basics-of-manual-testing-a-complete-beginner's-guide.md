## What is Manual Testing and Why It’s Still Relevant?
Manual testing involves rigorously executing test cases without automation. It adds value by simulating real-user behaviors that coding scripts often miss. Dive into its significance for user interface design, small-scale projects, and adaptive environments.

### Benefits of Manual Testing:
- Simulating real-life scenarios
- Boosting early QA in development
- Saving costs for smaller projects
