## Manual Testers in an AI-Driven Workflow

AI is revolutionizing Quality Assurance with predictive algorithms and user simulation tools. However, manual testers offer expertise AI cannot replicate, such as:

1. **User Experience Testing:** Human intuition ensures applications meet aesthetic and emotional user expectations.
2. **Exploratory Testing:** Beyond predefined models, manual testers uncover unanticipated errors.
3. **Real-Time Adaptability:** Human analysts quickly test unpredictable or evolving application features.
