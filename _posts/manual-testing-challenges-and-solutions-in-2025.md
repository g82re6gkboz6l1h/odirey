## Top Challenges in Manual Testing

### 1. **Time Pressures**
Tight Agile and DevOps cycles demand rapid testing while manual evaluation takes time, making it hard for testers to keep pace.

### 2. **Complex Applications**
Modern apps integrating AI, cloud services, and IoT require robust testing skills across diverse platforms and more backend intricacy.

### 3. **Human Error**
Subjectivity and cognitive strain undermine accuracy, risking missed defects and inconsistent test execution.

### 4. **Evolving Technology**
The rise of ML, blockchain, and IoT requires testers to learn specialized frameworks to ensure seamless test coverage.

### 5. **Integrating Automation**
Balancing exploratory manual testing with automation introduces challenges in workflows, testing accuracy, and collaboration dynamics.
