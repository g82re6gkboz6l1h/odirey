## Why Manual Testing Remains Essential

AI has limitations when it comes to creativity, empathy, and subjective understanding—qualities only humans possess. Key areas relying on manual testing include:

1. **Exploratory Testing**: Human intuition and adaptability enable testers to identify unpredictable issues.
2. **User Experience Testing**: Assessing subjective aspects like accessibility and navigational ease requires empathy.
3. **Ethical AI Testing**: Manual testers ensure algorithms avoid bias and adhere to ethical standards.
4. **Contextual Testing**: Industry-specific and cultural nuances often escape AI detection.
