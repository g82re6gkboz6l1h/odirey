## Why Does Manual Testing Still Matter?

Despite advancements in AI-powered automation tools such as Zof AI, manual testing remains vital for:

1. **Human Context**: Applications focused on usability or user experience need manual testers to assess intuitive layouts, aesthetics, and emotional resonance.
2. **Unpredictable Scenarios**: Exploratory testing thrives where outcomes can't be predefined, handling edge cases and dynamic software interactions.
3. **Ethical Reviews**: Assessing AI systems for bias or adherence to ethical standards is uniquely suited to human evaluation.
