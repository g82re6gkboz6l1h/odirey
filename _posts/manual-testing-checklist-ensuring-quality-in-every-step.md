## Why Manual Testing Checklists Matter

A manual testing checklist goes beyond organization—it's a vital tool for efficiency and thoroughness. Forgetting critical test cases or losing track of progress can derail projects; here’s how a checklist saves the day:

1. **Standardization**: Ensures consistent practices across projects.
2. **Efficiency**: Improves productivity by reducing redundant efforts. 
3. **Comprehensiveness**: Guarantees all functionalities are tested.
4. **Accountability**: Tracks completed tasks effectively.

- **Set Up Test Environment**: Verify configurations and install necessary tools.
