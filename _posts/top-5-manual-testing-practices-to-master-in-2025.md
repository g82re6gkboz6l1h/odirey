## 1. User-Centric Focus: Testing for Real-World Usability

### Why User-Centric Testing Matters
User-centric software thrives on intuitive and accessible experiences. Manual testers must adopt a user-first mindset, ensuring ease of use and seamless functionality. Key areas to focus on include:

- **Usability Testing:** Pinpoint navigation or design challenges.
- **Behavioral Analysis:** Observe realistic user interactions with software applications.

Stay ahead in 2025 by mastering empathy in testing, enabling enhanced user stories and scenarios.
