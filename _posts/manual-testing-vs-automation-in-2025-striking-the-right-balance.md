## Manual Testing: Strengths and Challenges

Manual testing remains indispensable for areas requiring human creativity and intuition, such as exploratory testing and UI workflows.

### Strengths:
- **Human Intuition:** Detects real-world bugs automated scripts miss.
- **Context Simulation:** Spots unique edge cases.
- **Flexibility:** Adapts quickly to changing requirements.

### Limitations:
- Time-consuming.
- Less scalable for high-volume testing.
- Prone to human error after repetitive cycles.

---

Automation: Benefits and Drawbacks

Automation testing excels at repetitive processes while improving speed and ensuring broad test coverage. However, it has its challenges when applied to nuanced scenarios.

### Strengths:
- **Efficiency:** Rapid execution of repetitive tasks.
- **Scalability:** Covers complex datasets at scale.
- **Consistency:** Eliminates risks of human error.

### Limitations:
- **Limited creativity:** Cannot handle complex contextual issues.
- **Initial cost:** Requires significant investment in tools and setup.
