## Understanding Manual and Automated Testing

Manual testing utilizes human testers to execute various test cases without depending on scripts, offering creativity and adaptability. It’s crucial for areas such as exploratory and usability tests. Automated testing leverages tools to efficiently handle repetitive tests, offering scalability and accuracy.

| **Feature**           | **Manual Testing**                     | **Automated Testing**                  |
|-----------------------|-----------------------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| **Speed**             | Slower                                | Faster with tools                     |
| **Cost Over Time**    | High for repetitive tasks             | Economical for recurring test cases   |
| **Best Use Cases**    | Usability, exploratory, creative tests | Regression, load, scalability tests   |

## Why Manual Testing Is Still Crucial in 2025

### a) **Human Insight Matters**
Complex systems like AR/VR applications require judgment beyond programmed scripts.

### b) **Adaptability in Exploratory Testing**
Ad-hoc scenarios often uncover hidden bugs.

### c) **Early-Stage Development**
For startups, manual testing is cost-effective and practical initially.
