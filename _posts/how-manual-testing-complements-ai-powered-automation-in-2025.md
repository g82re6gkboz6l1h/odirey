This article explores how the synergy of manual testing and automation ensures robust, user-focused software. Learn why manual testing outshines automation in exploratory testing, usability testing, and high-stakes scenarios, supported by real-world case studies and the powerful role of platforms like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai).
